Our Honeycomb Packaging Solutions

Honeycomb Paper Sheets & Rolls

Expandable kraft paper honeycomb wrap available in sheets and rolls. Perfect for wrapping, cushioning, and void fill applications.

  • Multiple widths and lengths
  • Expands up to 40x original size
  • Natural kraft paper color
  • Food-safe and non-toxic

Honeycomb Cardboard Panels

Rigid, lightweight structural panels with exceptional strength-to-weight ratio. Ideal for protective inserts and dividers.

  • Various thicknesses
  • Customizable dimensions
  • High compression strength
  • Easy to cut and shape

Honeycomb Edge Protectors

L-shaped and U-shaped corner guards providing superior edge protection during shipping and handling.

  • L-shape and U-shape profiles
  • Various lengths and thicknesses
  • Stackable and recyclable
  • Reusable for multiple shipments
